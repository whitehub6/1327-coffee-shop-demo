Our Story

More Than a Cup —
It's an Experience

At 1327 Coffee Shop, we believe coffee is a craft, not a commodity. Tucked inside the vibrant Rustomjee Global City in Virar West, our intimate space was born from a simple obsession: making every cup exceptional.

Our menu is sweet and short — deliberately curated so that everything on it is something we're proud of. From rich espressos to flavorful snacks, every item is crafted with precision and love. Our brownie? People come from across Virar just for it.
