Since Opening

The Safe Space Sanctuary

We believe that a great cup of coffee is only as good as the community surrounding it.

1327 Coffee Shop was founded as a safe harbor for creators, students, and neighbors in Virar West. Located directly opposite Rustomjee International School, we wanted to build a place where you can pause, connect, and feel fully welcome.

Every single espresso shot is pulled from fresh dark-roasted beans, our sourdough breads are butter-grilled fresh on the griddle, and our space is designed with cozy, intimate corners so you can study, play board games, or disconnect entirely.
